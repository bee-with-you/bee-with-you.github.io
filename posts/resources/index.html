Support Groups

Facebook support groups

One needs to search the following keyworkds in Facebook to find them, and choose to join the interested ones.

  • Hypospadias Parent Support Group (Pro-surgery and Pro-choice)

  • Hypospadias and Epispadias Association (HEA)

  • Hypospadias & Penile Birth Defect Support

  • Hypospadias Surgery Support

  • Real Support & Education for People with Hypospadias/Epispadias/Chordee

  • Dr. Snodgrass/Dr. Bush Hypospadias Support Group

Reddit Support Groups

One can search the keyword of interest to find related groups to join, and the following one is so far the most related group.

  • r/hypospadias
